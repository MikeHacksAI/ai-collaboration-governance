## Plan: Workspace Open/Close & On-Demand Automation

**Summary:**  
Your project requires robust automation on workspace open, close, and on-demand triggers to ensure governance, audit, and drift logging are always up-to-date, recruiter/audit-ready, and compliant with your formatting/timestamp rules. Several automation requirements are documented, but some are not fully working or enforced.

---

**Steps to Automate (per requirements and TODO):**

### 1. On Workspace Open/Close (and on user request)
- **Scan for new files** in incidents, drift-log, and ai-chat-history (including subfolders).
- **For each new file** (HTML or markdown):
  - Create a recruiter-audit-ready summary in the appropriate log (filename matches source).
  - Summarize Microsoft chat history files and log findings.
  - Log every governance, incident, and drift finding for each untracked file.
  - Mark processed files and update TODO.md to reflect completed automation.
- **Update audit summary and tracking documentation** (`audit-summary.md`, incident-log.md, drift-log-cumulative.md).
- **Maintain append-only, timestamped, and deduplicated logs** (reverse chronological order, full `YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM` format).
- **Auto-open** AI-Collaboration-Handoff.md for session continuity.

### 2. On User Request for Logging/Review
- **Confirm** in chat that both drift-log.md and drift-log-cumulative.md were updated.
- **Log any drift or missed requirement automatically** (silent logging if caught by automation, explicit confirmation if user-initiated).
- **Reference and cross-link** chat history, incident, and drift files in summary logs.

---

**What is NOT working or missing (per requirements and logs):**
- Processed files are not always marked or reflected in TODO.md.
- Audit summary and tracking documentation (`audit-summary.md`) is sometimes missing or not updated.
- Deduplication and standardization (using `convert-drift-entry.ps1`) may not be consistently applied.
- Not all logs are visually engaging or use required markdown formatting (emojis, headers).
- Some automation steps (like recruiter-audit-ready summaries for every new file) may require manual intervention or are not fully automated.

---

**Relevant files to automate or update:**
- incidents, drift-log, ai-chat-history (scan for new files)
- drift-log.md, drift-log-cumulative.md, incident-log.md, `audit-summary.md`
- TODO.md (mark processed, update status)
- AI-Collaboration-Handoff.md (auto-open, session continuity)
- project-requirements.md (for formatting/timestamp rules)
- `convert-drift-entry.ps1` (for deduplication/standardization)
